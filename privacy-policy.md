# Privacy Policy for Shopee Reviews Scraper
**Last updated:** 24 Dec 2025
## Introduction
Shopee Reviews Scraper ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our Chrome Extension.
We generally do not collect, store, or share your personal data. The extension operates locally on your device to extract public information from Shopee product pages.
## Data Collection and Usage
### 1. User Data
We do **not** collect personal identification information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. We do not track your browsing history outside of the specific Shopee product pages you choose to scrape.
### 2. Scraped Data
The extension extracts public product reviews (e.g., ratings, comments, dates, anonymous usernames) displayed on Shopee product pages.
- **Processing:** All processing happens locally in your browser.
- **Storage:** The data is temporarily held in your browser's memory to generate the CSV/Excel file. We do not transmit this data to any external servers or databases owned by us.
- **Export:** You have full control over the downloaded data files.
### 3. Permissions
We request the following permissions to function:
- **activeTab / Host Permissions:** To access the content of the Shopee page you are currently viewing to extract reviews.
- **Scripting:** To inject the code required to scroll and read the review elements.
- **Storage:** To save your user preferences (such as file format settings) locally.
- **Downloads:** To save the extracted data as a file to your computer.
## Third-Party Services
This extension interacts with Shopee.com websites solely to read the data displayed on the page. We are not affiliated with Shopee. Please refer to Shopee's own privacy policy for how they handle their user data.
## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
